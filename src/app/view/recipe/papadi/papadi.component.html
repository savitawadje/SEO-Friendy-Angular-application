Papdi Pizza|Tea Time Snack




Papdi Pizza|Tea Time Snack is a simple, easy snack recipe where papris are spread with pizza sauce mixed with some toppings and then topped with shredded cheese and baked.This is a great finger food idea for kitty parties ,kids evening snacks or even for adults with some chai.


Ingredients

Papdis as required

  • 1/4 cup Pizza sauce
  • 2 tbsp onion,finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp tomato,finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp capsicum/bell pepper,finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp sweet corn,boiled
  • Mozzarella cheese grated as required
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1/4 tsp chilli flakes (optional)
  • salt as per taste

    • Direction
    1. In a bowl,add together pizza sauce,vegetables,pepper powder,chill flakes and salt ,mix well .
    2. Spread papdi on a table,Put a tablespoon of prepared mixture on each papdi.
    3. Then add grated mozzarella cheese on top of each papdi.
    4. Heat tawa on a low flame,apply some butter and place these papdi’s on the tawa,close the lid and cook on low flame until cheese melts(takes appx 3-4 minutes).OR
    5. Transfer papdi’s on a butter paper lined baking tray and bake in a preheated oven at 180 °C/360 F (appx takes 3-5 minutes) till the cheese ,melts.
    6. move and Place the papdi on a serving platter and sprinkle some sev on top. Serve immediately garnished with coriander sprigs.
